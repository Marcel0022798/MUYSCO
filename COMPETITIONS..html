Introduction to MANCO COMPETITIONS

MANCO COMPETITIONS was created in 2022 by Marcel Nasson, with a bold vision: to seek out the best people who can make powerful speeches, inspire audiences, and actively participate in YCS competitions across East Africa. From its inception, the competition has been more than just a contest. it has been a platform for discovering talent, encouraging participation, and building a community of individuals who are passionate about communication, innovation, and unity.

The Founding Purpose

The competition was born out of a desire to give people a stage where their voices could be heard and their ideas valued. Marcel Nasson recognized that East Africa is full of talented individuals who often lack opportunities to showcase their skills. By creating MANCO COMPETITIONS, he sought to bridge that gap, offering a structured and fair environment where participants could demonstrate their abilities in speechmaking, problem‑solving, and creative thinking.

Our Vision and Mission

The vision of MANCO COMPETITIONS extends far beyond East Africa. It is about equipping people both within Africa and outside the continent with the knowledge and skills necessary to thrive in today’s interconnected world. We believe that competitions should not only test ability but also nurture growth. Our vision emphasizes several key pillars:

Speaking and Communication Skills: The ability to express ideas clearly and persuasively is one of the most valuable skills in any field. MANCO COMPETITIONS encourages participants to refine their public speaking, presentation, and communication abilities.

Innovation and Creativity: Through various criteria of science, talent, and problem‑solving, participants are challenged to think outside the box, to innovate, and to propose solutions that can make a real difference.

Unity and Cooperation: Competitions should bring people together, not divide them. MANCO COMPETITIONS fosters collaboration, teamwork, and mutual respect among participants from diverse backgrounds.

Knowledge Sharing: By participating, individuals gain exposure to new ideas, perspectives, and experiences, enriching their understanding and broadening their horizons.


A Warm Welcome to Participants

We warmly welcome everyone who is ready to take part in this exciting journey. Whether you are a student eager to showcase your skills, a professional looking to challenge yourself, or simply someone passionate about communication and innovation, MANCO COMPETITIONS is your platform.

Here, you will find encouragement, fairness, and opportunity. We invite you to bring your best energy, creativity, and determination. This competition is not just about winning prizes—it is about learning, connecting, and being part of a movement that values talent and unity.

Areas of Competition

To ensure a rich and diverse experience, MANCO COMPETITIONS includes several categories where participants can demonstrate their abilities:

Typing Skills – Speed, accuracy, and efficiency in digital communication are tested here. In today’s world, typing is more than a technical skill; it is a gateway to productivity and professionalism.

Smart Speeches – Participants deliver powerful, well‑structured speeches that inspire, persuade, and captivate audiences. This category highlights the importance of clarity, confidence, and charisma in communication.

Best Idea to Solve the Jam – Innovation is at the heart of this category. Participants are challenged to present creative solutions to real‑world problems, demonstrating their ability to think critically and propose actionable ideas.

Deep Interviews – This category focuses on thoughtful, articulate interviews that showcase confidence, clarity, and professionalism. It tests the ability to engage meaningfully, respond intelligently, and leave a lasting impression.

Each of these areas reflects the core values of MANCO COMPETITIONS: communication, innovation, and excellence.





Why MANCO COMPETITIONS Matters???

Competitions are more than contests; they are catalysts for growth. MANCO COMPETITIONS is designed to achieve several important goals: Encourage bilingualism and communication excellence by promoting strong speaking and writing skills.
